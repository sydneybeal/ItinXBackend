Today you will be transferred to the Cape Town airport for your commercial flight to Johannesburg. Commercial flights not included in land costs. All meals, local drinks (excluding premium import brands), and shared game activities are included in your stay. This afternoon, meet your guide and other guests for a shared game activity.
